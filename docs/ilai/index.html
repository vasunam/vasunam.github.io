Why Stovetop Matters.

1. The Boil

The heat is essential. Boiling extracts the crucial oils and bold flavors from the whole spices, not just the color from the tea.

2. The Aeration

Pouring or 'pulling' the chai back and forth adds air, cooling it slightly and creating a velvety smooth texture you can't get otherwise.

3. The Sip

The final result: Smooth, spicy, and soul-warming. A flavor that is grounded in ritual and ready for the day ahead.
